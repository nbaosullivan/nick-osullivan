## Pre-start

### Prerequisites

* 12v electrical system
* Mobile router with internet access

### Resources needed

* Raspberry PI 4 Model B 4GB
* Ethernet cable
* SD card (32GB minimum)
* (TODO power cable)
* Computer with <https://www.balena.io/etcher/> to flash the SD card
